[I want to set a character's stats and starting equipment]

[Goal]
Set Wolfar's (the blue character's) initial attack power and HP to 999, and remove his starting equipment.

[1]
Click the Changeable Database
to open the window shown on the right.
[2]
Confirm that the "Type" shows "Player Character Status".

(If not, click "0: Player Character Status".)
[3]
Look to the right — a list like the one shown will appear.
Wolfar should already be selected.

Set both "HP" and "Max HP" to 999. (They are currently 40 and 30.)

Similarly, set "Base Attack Power" to 999. (Currently 15.)
[4]
Next, click the "Page 2" button near the top.
The list will change to what is shown on the right.

Find any entries that are not already set to "[-1]: <No Equipment>"
— namely "Equipped Weapon" and "Equipped Armor 2" — click them,
and change them to "[-1]: <No Equipment>".
[5]
Run Test Play and confirm
that the attack power and HP are now 999 and there is no equipment.
