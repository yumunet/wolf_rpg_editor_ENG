【Shift the Entire Map】

[Goal]

When creating a map, you may sometimes want to shift the entire map.
For example, when you increase the map size and want to add more to the empty space,
this technique will be useful if you are adding content to the top or left side.
Note that events are moved along with the map, making this very convenient.

【Steps】

[1] First, open a map

Open the dungeon map.
Let's try shifting this map upward a little.

Using this icon () to display the map at 2x zoom
makes the effect easier to understand (click "1/2", the second from the left).

[2] The clever trick for shifting a map!

Hold the Shift key and Ctrl key, then press the arrow keys.
You will see the entire map, including events, shift (in this example, we pressed Up once).
Also, on the side opposite to the direction you shifted (e.g. the bottom when shifting up),
the parts that went off the edge of the map should reappear.

[3] Try shifting further

If you keep holding Shift and Ctrl and pressing the arrow keys,
you will see that the parts that went off the edge wrap around to the opposite side.
Pressing Up repeatedly shifts the map further and further in the up direction.
(The screenshot on the right shows the result of shifting the map significantly upward.)

Note that the event contact range (the red border) is centered on the original event,
so only part of the range will not appear on the opposite side.

That's all for this tutorial. Thank you.
