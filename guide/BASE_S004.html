[I want to add new player characters]

[Goal]
　Add a new player character and save the data.


[1]
　Click the Changeable Database
　to open the window shown on the right.
　　
[2]
　Confirm that the "Type" shows "Player Character Status".

　(If not, click "0: Player Character Status".)
[3]　
　Click one of the empty
　"Data" slots.
　
[4]　
　Enter the character name.
　Other fields can be filled in later.

※ For how to configure the other fields,
　see the related articles in the <Settings> section.
　　

[5]
　Press the Update button or OK button.
　The new player character has been saved.
