WOLF RPG Editor Perfect Guide

　This site is a guide that lists the things beginners want to do and the obstacles they are likely to encounter when first using WOLF RPG Editor,
　and describes the specific steps to accomplish those things.

　※This guide was written based on WOLF RPG Editor Ver2, so some screenshots and features described
　　may differ from the latest version.
