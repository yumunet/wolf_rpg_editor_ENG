[Handover from the WOLF RPG Editor Official Guide Writing Community]

As of December 21, 2013, activities in the "WOLF RPG Editor Official Guide Writing Community" have concluded.

Outstanding issues and handover items remaining after the conclusion of activities are recorded here.

[Topics Placed on Hold for Writing]

The following topics were originally planned for revision or writing at the start of the project, but due to the scope becoming too large for revision or writing,
and because the official manual and other sites provide sufficient supplementary information,
revision and writing of these topics in the guide writing community has been placed on hold.
They are listed here as items for consideration should revision or writing become necessary.

[Closing]

Writing topics is something done in the gaps between daily study, work, and one's own creative activities,
and is by no means an easy task.
Furthermore, creating a manual is more difficult work than one might imagine.

That we were able to advance this community project as far as we did is due to the efforts of
Manami, the founder of the "WOLF RPG Editor Official Guide Writing Community" and the main author,
and Hdk103-Arch, who participated from the community's early days and handled particularly difficult topics to write.
We believe it was thanks to both of their contributions.

We also received environmental support from SmokingWOLF, the creator of WOLF RPG Editor,
for which we are grateful.

In addition, many people participated in writing the guide and helped establish the foundation for writing it,
and we feel that it was thanks to their support that we were able to reach this milestone.
We express our sincere gratitude here. Thank you very much.
