【I Want to Scroll the Map More Easily】

[Goal]

　When creating maps in WOLF RPG Editor, small maps are manageable, but
as the map size grows larger, scrolling can become quite tedious.
　Here we introduce a way to scroll even large maps with ease.

【Steps】

[1] First, let's open "Sample Map A" from the sample game.

Use the Map Selector to switch to "Sample Map A".
Even with the window maximized, the map probably won't be fully visible.

Normally, you would use the scroll bar on the right side of the window or the mouse wheel for vertical scrolling,
and the scroll bar at the bottom of the window for horizontal scrolling.
That still works, but when the map is extremely large, it can become quite tedious.

[2] The mouse wheel is the key!

Use the mouse wheel (or middle button) — the red part shown in the image on the right.
Confirm that the cursor is on the map, then try pressing and holding the wheel while dragging.
(It works regardless of which layer is currently selected, by the way.)

[3] Did it work?

How about that? Did the map scroll in the direction you moved the mouse?
Now release the wheel. The scrolling will stop there.

With this, you can scroll even an enormous map with ease.

【Appendix】

If the method above doesn't work... (Ver1.31 and later)

Some users may not have a wheel on their mouse, or may be using a touchpad on a laptop,
making the method in [2] above unavailable.

But don't worry. If WOLF RPG Editor itself is Ver1.31 or later, you can achieve the same result by holding the Space key and moving the mouse.
Note that the mouse cursor takes the shape of a hand at that time.
......It might even work with a pen tablet too.

That's all for this tutorial. Great work!
......Actually, this tip is written clearly as a comment in the "the Professor" event in Basic System Ver1.43 and later!
